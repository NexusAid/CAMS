Student Guide

Welcome to the CAMS Student Portal. This guide will help you navigate the system and participate in campus clubs.

1. Getting Started: Registration and Login
  • How to Register: Navigate to the CAMS homepage and click on the Register button. Fill in the required details (Full Name, Email, Registration Number) and create your account.
  • How to Log In: Use your registered email address and password on the student login page.
2. Joining and Participating in Clubs
  • How to Join a Club: From your Dashboard, click on Available Clubs. Browse the directory, view club details, and click Request to Join. Your request will remain pending until a Club Leader approves it.
  • My Memberships: You can track all your active, pending, and past club memberships from the My Memberships section.
3. Participating in Club Elections
  • How to Nominate Yourself: Navigate to the Elections section during an open nomination window. Select a position and submit your application. Note: Final year students may not be eligible to run for office.
  • How to Vote: During an active voting window, open the election, select your preferred candidates for each position, and click Cast Vote. Remember, your vote is secure and anonymous.
