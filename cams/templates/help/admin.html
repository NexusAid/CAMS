Admin Guide

Welcome to the CAMS Admin Portal. This guide details the processes for overseeing club activities and ensuring university compliance.

1. Club Approvals & Compliance
  • Approving Clubs: Review new club registrations and approve them once they meet all the initial criteria.
  • Monitoring Compliance: Use the dashboard to monitor the compliance status of all clubs. You can issue warnings or revoke status if clubs fall behind on their requirements.
2. Managing Events
  • Event Reviews: Review event proposals submitted by Club Leaders. Ensure they align with university policies before approving.
  • Calendar Oversight: Monitor the global event calendar to prevent scheduling conflicts.
3. Elections & Audits
  • Election Setup: Open and manage election cycles for clubs. Oversee candidate applications and verify final results.
  • Financial Audits: Review submitted financial reports and conduct audits to maintain financial transparency among clubs.
