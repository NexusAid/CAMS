Club Leader Guide

Welcome to the CAMS Club Leader Portal. This guide will help you manage your club operations effectively.

1. Managing Memberships
  • Reviewing Applications: Navigate to the Members section to view pending membership requests. You can approve or reject them to maintain your club's roster.
  • Member Directory: View all active members and track their participation status.
2. Planning Events
  • Creating Events: Use the Events module to propose new activities. Fill out the event details, required resources, and submit it for Admin approval.
  • Tracking Attendance: Keep track of who attends your events directly within the platform.
3. Compliance and Documents
  • Document Uploads: Submit your club constitution, minutes, and financial reports through the Documents tab.
  • Compliance Status: Regularly check your club's compliance score to ensure you meet all university guidelines.
