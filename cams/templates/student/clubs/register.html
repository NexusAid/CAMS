    University Regulations

    By registering this club, you agree to comply with all Egerton University rules and regulations, including:

    • Maintaining at least 20 active student members
    • Retaining an active staff patron (lecturer level or above)
    • Submitting periodic financial and activity reports
    • Holding annual, fair, and transparent elections
    • Requesting prior approval for off-campus or major events
