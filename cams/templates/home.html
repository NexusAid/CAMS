What We Offer

Everything you need to run clubs effectively

From compliance to events, CAMS covers the full lifecycle of student club management.

Compliance Tracking

Automated monitoring of club regulations, minimum members, and document submissions with real-time notifications.

Event Management

Plan, schedule, and manage club events with approval workflows, attendance tracking, and budget management.

Document Management

Secure storage of constitutions, minutes, financial reports, and important documents with version control.

Member Management

Efficiently manage club memberships, track member activities, and maintain records of all participants.

Financial Tracking

Monitor club finances, track budgets, manage expenses, and generate comprehensive financial reports.

Analytics & Reports

Generate detailed reports on club performance, member engagement, and event success metrics.
